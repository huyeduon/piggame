On your turn, you roll a single six-sided die and add the number rolled to your turn total. After each roll, you have two choices: Continue rolling the die to accumulate more points for your turn total. Choose to "Hold," which means your turn total is added to your overall score, and your turn ends. There is a risk involved in continuing to roll. If you roll a 1, your turn total is reset to zero, and your turn immediately ends, forfeiting any points you might have accumulated in that turn. The next player then takes their turn. The first player to reach or exceed 100 points in their overall score wins the game.
